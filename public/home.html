Your Instructors

Founder & Lead Instructor

Basma

Voice · Songwriting · Performance · Social Media

Born and raised in Las Vegas. UNLV graduate, vocalist, puppeteer, and creative entrepreneur. Basma leads the free summer program — the place where kids get their first taste of music, performance, and dance. She started BASMA to give every kid in Vegas a chance to discover music.

Co-Founder & Studio Instructor

Sarah

Viola · Violin · Piano · Music Theory

Classically trained in viola, violin, cello, and piano. Sarah leads the professional intensive training at the Tropicana studio — the place where kids who loved the free classes go to get serious. Real personal attention and performance opportunities.
